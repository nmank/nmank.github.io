Nathan
Mankovich

PhD in mathematics, currently a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Image Processing Laboratory at the University of Valencia under Gustau Camps-Valls. Research focuses on dynamic mode decomposition, flag manifolds for modern data analysis, and dimensionality reduction — with applications to climate modeling and machine learning.
